About This Site

I’m a music box maniac. My bedroom is filled with gaudy snowglobes and statuettes with mechanical musical movements. I love to wind them all at the same time. One music box is like a spoonful of honey. With twenty music boxes playing, you’ve got the honey, the hives, and all the bees! I made this website so that everyone can hear this orchestra of sweetness. Let ‘er rip!
